My Main Setup

This setup is where I spend most of my time. Also this is my most high powered machine and generally my best setup.

Computer:

  • Motherboard: Asrock B450m Pro 4
  • CPU: Ryzen 5 2600
  • Cooler: Hyper 212 (Black Edition)
  • RAM: Corsair Vengence LPX 32GB Black (4X8)
  • GPU: Nvidia RTX 2060
  • PSU: Corsair RM 750
  • Storage: 240GB SSD, 1TB SSD, 2TB HDD
  • Case: Corsair 4000D (White)

This computer I built myself, I upgrade it when I need to but at the moment it is fine. There is a dust filter that stops it from getting as dusty as it was in the last case.

Monitors:
